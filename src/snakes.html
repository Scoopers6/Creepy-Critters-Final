This is a juvenile milk snake found Upstate NY. This little guy was wandering in an area of the yard with heavy foot traffic, so our story submitter picked him up in a jar to move him. He was young so he hadn’t learned which places were and weren’t safe yet! Our submitter placed him in a mason jar as to not stress the little guy out too much by handling him. She took a few pictures to admire later before releasing him to a quieter, safer part of the yard.
